Capacitive Fill Sensor PCB

Designed for Berkeley STAR's competitive rocket team, this custom PCB enables precise fill-level sensing in cryogenic propellant tanks using capacitive measurement techniques optimized for extreme environments.

Technical Implementation

  • Designed a custom PCB for cryogenic tank fill-level sensing, leveraging the dielectric constant difference between air and kerosene/LOX to measure capacitance between electrodes
  • Integrated ESP32-S3 microcontroller, FDC1004 capacitive measurement IC, and RS485 communication circuit for reliable, noise-resistant data transmission to DAQ systems
  • Implemented power regulation (LDO) and electrode connection with FDC1004 for precise picoFarad-scale capacitance measurements
  • Produced complete schematic, PCB layout, and bill of materials in Altium, delivering a manufacturable board
  • Currently working on testing/writing software
